The Beginning

It all started with Ammaji. In the bustling lanes of Varanasi, her rooftop was a kingdom of ceramic jars (barnis) basking in the summer sun. Neighbors would trace the aroma of mustard oil and fenugreek to our doorstep, often leaving with a small bowl of her signature Mango Achar.

She didn't use scales or measuring cups. Her hands knew the "andaz" (estimation) perfectly. A pinch of kalonji here, a handful of saunf there. This wasn't just cooking; it was chemistry powered by intuition and love.

Why We Are Different

Farm-to-Jar

We don't buy from middlemen. We source raw mangoes from Ratnagiri and spices from the Khari Baoli market.

Slow Aged

No artificial heat. Our pickles are aged under the natural sun for at least 21 days to develop complex flavors.
